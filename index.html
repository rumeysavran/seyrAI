Frequently asked questions

Got a specific question? Contact us

How can AI consulting help my business?

We help you identify where AI can save time, reduce errors, and improve decisions — from workflows and documents to data analytics and customer touchpoints. Every engagement is tailored to your goals and existing tools.

Is AI difficult to integrate?

We design solutions to fit into your current systems. Integration complexity depends on your stack; we handle the technical work and train your team so you can maintain and extend over time.

What industries do you work with?

We work with startups, mid-size companies, and enterprises across sectors — including tech, finance, healthcare, retail, and professional services — wherever AI can improve operations and decision-making.

Do I need technical knowledge?

No. We focus on outcomes and usability. Your team gets clear documentation, training, and support so you can use and evolve your AI solutions without deep technical expertise.

What kind of support do you offer?

We provide email and chat support, documentation, and ongoing help as you run and extend your AI systems. For larger engagements we can include a dedicated contact and priority response. We also run AI training and workshops so your team can build confidence with AI.
